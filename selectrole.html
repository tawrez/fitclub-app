FitClub Gym management

How do you want to use FitClub?

Choose whether you’re joining as a gym member or managing a gym. You can always switch or add more gyms later.

For individuals

Sign up as gym member

Perfect if you’re working out at a FitClub‑powered gym.

  • Renew your membership from the app, no queues.
  • Track check‑ins and plan expiry dates.
  • Refer friends and earn rewards on yearly plans.
Best for members who want a smooth experience.
For gyms & studios

Sign up as gym owner

Designed for owners, managers and franchise partners.

  • Automated renewal reminders and member lists.
  • Daily / weekly / monthly earning reports.
  • Manage trainers, plans and multiple branches.
Best for owners who want less admin work.
