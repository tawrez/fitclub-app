Welcome

FitClub keeps your front desk, trainers and members on the same page — without registers, notebooks or manual follow‑ups.

  • Owners see everything: members, plans, payments and renewals in one dashboard.
  • Members manage their entire journey from the app: join, renew, refer and pay.

For gym owners

No more “who has to renew?”

FitClub automatically keeps track of every active, expired and upcoming membership so you never miss a renewal.

  • Automatic reminders for expiring members via app notifications and SMS (optional).
  • Clear list of members to renew today, this week and this month.
  • In‑app renewals: members can pay from the app, no cash counter rush.
Result: Higher renewals with less follow‑up calls and manual checking.
